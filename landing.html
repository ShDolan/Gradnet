About

GradNet is a new generation interface designed to connect graduates with employers in a seemless environment. It provides state of the art application control and analytics to employers and recruiters while allowing candidates to display their best qualities to get the best chance of finding employment.

GradNet was founded in 2018 by five computer science postgraduates who found traditional jobhunting sites to be outdated and unaproachable. Most job posts alianated graduates by demanding 'experience', something that seemed to be overlooked as companies eventually filled such positions with graduates. GradNet's vision is to create an environment where undergraduates are welcomed, advertised to, and recruited.

Thus, the GradNet student profile is very academia centric, allowing the recruiters to better distinguish good talent in absence of experience markers.

News

GradNet launches new site

April 28th, 2018.

The GradNet team are proud to launch their newly developed website. The easy to navigate, minimalist design features all of the functionality required for users to create accounts and get started. Employers/recruiters have the option to make job openings posts, search through and review applications, accept or decline applicants, and even advanced search through students registered on the site to find candidates with specific desirable credentials in order to head-hunt more successfuly. Students can advanced search through job listings, as well as employers that may be of interest to them, and apply for the jobs posted by employers or recruiters. Both parties can communicate with each other through a internal private messaging system.
