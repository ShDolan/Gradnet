    About

    GradNet is a new generation interface designed to connect graduates with employers in a seemless environment. It provides state of the art application control and analytics to employers and recruiters while allowing candidates to display their best qualities to get the best chance of finding employment.

    GradNet was founded in 2018 by five computer science postgraduates who found traditional jobhunting sites to be outdated and unaproachable. Most job posts alianated graduates by demanding 'experience', something that seemed to be overlooked as companies eventually filled such positions with graduates. GradNet's vision is to create an environment where undergrades are welcomed, advertised to, and recruited.

    Thus, the GradNet student profile is very academia centric, allowing the recruiters to better distinguish good talent.
